🌿 Plant of the Day

🪴 Aloe Vera

Healing burns, skin care, and digestion.

  • 💧 Water: Every 2–3 weeks
  • 🌞 Light: Indirect sunlight
  • 🪨 Soil: Well-drained cactus mix

🌱 Snake Plant

Air purification and low maintenance indoor care.

  • 💧 Water: Every 2–4 weeks
  • 🌤️ Light: Low to bright indirect
  • 🪴 Soil: Free-draining potting mix

🌸 Peace Lily

Beautiful air purifier with calming white blooms.

  • 💧 Water: Weekly or when soil is dry
  • 🌥️ Light: Filtered light
  • 🌱 Soil: Moist, well-drained soil
